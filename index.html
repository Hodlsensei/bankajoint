Frequently Asked Questions

Is BankaJoint really free?

Yes. Joining BankaJoint is currently free. However, membership is subject to approval to maintain quality and trust within the community.

Who can apply to join BankaJoint?

BankaJoint is open to:

  • Current Nigerian bank staff
  • Professionals working within the banking sector
  • Individuals seeking growth, accountability, and strong professional networks

Applicants outside the banking ecosystem will not be approved.

How does the application process work?

After applying:

  • Your details are reviewed
  • Applicants receive an invite to the community
  • Unsuccessful applications may be reconsidered in the future

What activities happen inside the community?

BankaJoint focuses on:

  • Curated growth webinars and events
  • Community accountability check-ins during designated periods
  • Two signature community tours each year

We intentionally avoid noise and random discussions.

Are there membership dues or hidden fees?

No membership dues at any point. Some activities (such as tours or dinners) will be pay-as-you-go.

Are the tours compulsory?

No. Community tours are optional experiences designed for bonding and relaxation. Members choose what fits their schedule and convenience.

What do you mean by "ethical income opportunities"?

These are optional opportunities shared within the community, such as:

  • Vetted job opportunities
  • Skill-building bootcamps for bankers
  • Structured side-income masterclasses

Is BankaJoint a job placement or recruitment platform?

No. BankaJoint is a community, not a job board or recruitment agency. However, we will occasionally share job opportunities that suit our members.

How can I contact the BankaJoint team?

You can reach us via WhatsApp at 08134444693 or through the official community after joining.
